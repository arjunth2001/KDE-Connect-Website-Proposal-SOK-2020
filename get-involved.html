Get Involved.

Hello,

Welcome to the KDE Family. KDE Connect is a perfect project to start contributing to KDE. You will need a basic understanding of programming concepts, the rest can be learned by doing. Experience with Android or Qt is beneficial but not needed. Not a programmer? We have other roles too. You can also contribute to other KDE Projects and Groups. We want to make it easy to get involved. Various other antlers are ready to help out new contributors. You can find them on the mentoring page.  Many of them hangout in chatrooms and Telegram. Feel free to ask them any question you may have!

Hey, Developer. Help Develop KDE Connect.  

We have a group to discuss development. You can access it from Telegram, IRC or matrix.org. Feel free to ask any questions there. We also have a mailing list. 

Junior Jobs

There are a couple of tasks marked as Junior Jobs on our workboard. Those have some extra information on how to approach them that help you get started.

We always get asked about the libraries that are used in KDE Connect Development, specifically most of the time

  • Qt
  • KF5PulseAudioQt
  • KF5PeopleVCard
  • Kirigami2

The full Libraries list can be found in the CMakeLists.txt file and they are available at the official frameworks page or on git, github.

Not a Programmer?

Not a problem. There is plenty of other tasks that you can help us with to make KDE Connect and other KDE Family of products better even if you don't know any programming language. See some of other ways below.
